Our Story

From Humble Beginnings to Industry Leader

Founded in 2015, SmartFix Pro started with a simple vision: to provide homeowners with trustworthy, professional appliance repair services they could rely on. What began as a small team of three technicians has grown into a nationwide network of over 500 certified professionals.

Our journey has been driven by innovation and a commitment to excellence. We pioneered smart diagnostics, real-time technician tracking, and transparent pricing - features that have become industry standards.

Today, we're proud to serve over 50,000 customers annually, with a 98% satisfaction rate and coverage across all 50 states.
